The Sims



History



The Sims is a series of video games developed Maxis and published through EA. It was created by Will Wright. He set out to create a 'virtual dollhouse' after losing his home in a fire. He wanted to incorporate the experience of losing everything into a game.


The Sims



The Sims(2000) focuses on control the daily activites of one or more virtual people called Sims who speak a made up language called Simlish. Sims are customizable and players control them as they pursue career and relationship goals.

The Sims 2



The Sims 2 (2004) allows you to create your own Sims, families, neighborhoods, and homes. Sims now have goals, wants, and fears. Players can choose to fulfill or deny them producing positive and negative outcomes. Sims 2 has six stages of life: toddler, child, teen, young adult, adult, and elder.

The Sims 3



The Sims 3 (2009) follows the same premise as the previous games. This time, there is an open world to move around in with no loading screens. There's also a new tool called Create a Style which allows players to customize every items color, material, and design pattern.

The Sims 4



The Sims 4 (2014) is the latest installment in the Sims series. It receives a lot of negative reviews for its lack of depth and content. The Sims 4 has been adding items that were in previous games for free via patch releases.
